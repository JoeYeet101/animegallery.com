Shounen & Shoujo

-Shounen refers to the demographic that this type of anime targets, which is male viewers around the age range of 10-18.

-Shoujo is basically the same thing as Shonen but their target is female viewers around the age range of 10-18.

*Assassination classroom*

Summary : A powerful creature claims that within a year, Earth will be destroyed by him, but he offers mankind a chance by becoming a homeroom teacher where he teaches his students about how to kill him. An Assassination Classroom begins.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 47 episodes (season 1 -2)

Adaptation : Manga

Type : Series

*Demon Slayer*

Summary : A family is attacked by demons and the only remaining family members are Tanjiro and his sister, Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister.

Status : On Going (season 2 coming soon)

Episodes : 26 episodes + 1 movie

Adaptation : Manga

Type : Series / Movie

*Ao Haru Ride*

Summary : Futaba Yoshioka, was an attractive and popular middle-schooler, and now that she is a high schooler, she wants to reset everything- Her life and image. But what happens when a boy tells her he likes her, when she doesn't even feel the same.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 12

Adaptation : Manga

Type : Series

*Kaichou wa maid sama!*

Summary : Takumi Usui, the most popular boy in school found out that the female student council has a secret. He could destroy her reputation with her secret... or he could use her secret as an opportunity to get closer to her.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 26 episodes

Adaptation : Manga

Type : Series
