What is Anime?

      Well if you don't know then you're either living under a rock (it's 2020, c'mon) or you just don't like the internet. (Understandable, have a nice day) Anyways, anime is a hand-drawn and computer animation that's originated from Japan. (basically japanese animation)                                                         

Fun fact: Oten Shimokawa, Seitaro Kitayama, and Jun'ichi Kouchi were the first generation of                animators in the late 1910s, who were known as the "father's of anime"                                                         

The Anime Community Phrases:

  Weeaboo (Weeb)= A non-Japanese person who is obsessed with anime                   or Japanese culture and behaves in a                   stereotypically Japanese manner

  Otaku= A Japanese person who is normally obsessed anime and manga

  Manga= A Japanese styled comic

  Mangaka= Manga artist

  Doujinshi= Fan-made manga

  Seiyuu= Voice actor/actress in anime

  Gakuran= The uniform made for middle school and high school            students in Japan

  Baka= Dummy, Idiot, Stupid

  Urusai= Noisy/Shut up

  Kawaii= Cute

  Nani= what

  Chibi= Something small or shrunken that is normally cute

  Trap= A character that looks or dresses like the opposite gender

  Tsundere= A character that's very cold on the outside until they             are comfortable enough in the anime to show their             cute/soft side

  Yandere= A character that's obsessed with their love interest and            would often use violance or force to show their love >:)

  Kuudere= A cool headed character often seen with an emotionless            blank expression and deadpan voice

  Waifu= A term that simps use to indicate their favourite female          character

  Husbando= A term that simps use to indicate their favourite male             character

  Harem= A sub-genre where the protagonist is surrounded by a lot of          female/male characters that have feelings for the          protagonist

  Yaoi= Men romance

  Yuri= Women romance

  Shonen/Shoujo= Shounen:boys and Shoujo:girls (These terms are often                      used to differentiate between intended audiences)

  Shonen ai= Boy romance :D

  Shoujo ai= Girl romance :D
