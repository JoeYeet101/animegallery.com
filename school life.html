School Life

-The primary setting of the anime genre deals mostly with school and student life. (This type of anime is usually seen within the comedy genre, though there are some entries in drama as well.)

*My Hero Academia*

Summary : In world where there is Quirks, a superhuman ability an individual can possess, 80% of the population has it. However, Izuku Midoriya was unlucky enough to be born quirkless, especially since his dream is to be a hero. But that’s not enough to stop him from enrolling in one of the world’s most prestigious hero academies.

Status : On Going (season 5 coming soon)

Episodes : 88 Episodes ( season 1-4 ) + 3 Movie

Adaptation : Manga

Type : Series / Movie

*Himouto!! Umaru-chan*

Summary : Himouto! Umaru-chan is a cute story that follows the daily adventures of Umaru and Taihei, as they take care of and put up with each other the best they can, as well as the unbreakable bonds between friends and siblings.

Status : Completed ( season 1-2 )

Episodes : 24 ( season 1-2 )

Adaptation : Manga

Type : Series

*Asobi Asobase*

Summary : Whether it be failing to learn English, trying desperately to become popular, or getting caught by teachers at the wrong time, school life will never be boring when the girls of Asobi Asobase are up to their hilarious antics.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 12 Episodes

Adaptation : Manga

Type : Series
