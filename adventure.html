Adventure

-Adventure animes are about travelling around the world to find a certain place, object or being. (sometimes it escalates into the whole universe and/or other dimensions)

*Hunter X Hunter*

Summary: Gon Freecss aspires to be a Hunter to find his father, Ging Freecss, who left him at a young age. Throughout his journey, he meets new companions, enemies, and encounter many hardships and struggels.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 148 episodes

Adaptation : Manga

Type : Series

*Spirited Away*

Summary: During her family moves to the suburbs, Chihiro wanders into a world ruled by spirits. She journeys through the unfamiliar world in order to save her parents, who got turned into pigs and return home.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 1

Adaptation : Original

Type : Movie

*JoJo's Bizarre Adventure*

Summary: This anime is mainly about adventure's of the Joestar family, who are possessed with intense psychic power. (also known as Stands) Each member encounters many bizarre adventures throughout their short-lived lives.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 152 (season 1-5)

Adaptation : Manga

Type : series
