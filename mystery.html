Mystery

-The existence of a central enigma is where all Mystery animes can be found. Whether it’s an event, a place, or an item, there’s some sort of mystery surrounding the main casts.

*Tokyo Ghoul*

Summary : Ghoul, a being that feeds on human flesh. Ken Kaneki, a collage student, went on a date with a hot girl. It was a very VERY bad date, the girl tried to eat him. (don't trust hot girls, guys ;-;) Kaneki survived and discovers that he underwent a surgery that transformed him into a half-ghoul.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 48 Episodes (season 1-4)

Adaptation : Manga

Type : Series

*Hyouka*

Summary : Oreki at the request of his older sister, along with his fellow club members are trying to keep their Classic Literature Club from being abolished. They begin to solve various mysteries, both to help with their club and at Eru's requests.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 22

Adaptation : Novel

Type : Series

*Durarara!!*

Summary : Mikado Ryuugamine got an invitation from a friend, convincing him to move to Ikebukuro, Tokyo. Ordinary citizens like himself, along with Ikebukuro's most colorful inhabitants are mixed up in the commotion breaking out in their city.

Status : Completed

Episodes : 60 Episodes (season 1-4)

Adaptation : Light Novel

Type : Series
